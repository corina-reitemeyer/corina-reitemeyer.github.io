Sprint 4 - Introducing Te Whare Tapa Whā

My wellbeing plan using the Tā Mason Durie's Te Whare Tapa Whā model.

8 April 2024

My wellbeing plan

After exploring the 4 dimensions of Te Whare Tapa Whā, I’ll commit to the following activities (might add onto this list over time):

Taha Tinana (physical wellbeing)

  • Going to Pilates class 2x a week
  • Going for a short walk once a day (preferably around lunch)
  • Trying to stick to healthy snacks, instead of sugary ones

Taha Hinengaro (emotional and mental wellbeing)

  • Commit to some light reading or meditation before sleep
  • Trying to stick to healthy snacks, instead of sugary ones

Taha Wairua (spiritual wellbeing)

This one is tricky as I am not religious nor spiritual.

  • Teaching once a week to feel a sense of purpose and connectedness to what I'm passionate about

Taha Whānau (family wellbeing)

  • Making plans with friends at least once a week
  • Go to a dance social once a month (to connect with my dance family)
  • Getting in touch with my family at least once a week (they don’t live in the same city as me).

Whenua (land)

  • Go for a walk around Oriental Bay once a week
